01 About

I'm a veterinarian (DVM) and Master's researcher at the College of Veterinary Medicine, Konkuk University in Seoul, working in the Wildlife Health Laboratory. My research sits at the crossroads of One Health — investigating how pathogens emerge, circulate, and evolve across wildlife, livestock, and human populations.

Combining field surveillance, whole-genome sequencing, and phylogenetic and phylodynamic analysis, I study viruses and bacteria in hosts ranging from cinereous vultures and gulls to feral pigeons and wild boars — with the goal of understanding emergence risk before it becomes an outbreak. I currently lead a national research grant on the disease ecology of avian paramyxoviruses.
